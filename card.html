True Mole

The term "true mole" signifies those of the Talpidae family found in most parts of the North American, Asian, and European continents.

  • Diet: Primarily earthworms
  • Social Structure: Solitary
  • Length: 6 to 8 inches
  • Weight: 3 to 5 ounces

Moles are able to survive in low-oxygen environments and tolerate high levels of carbon dioxide, which suits their underground habitat. Contrary to popular belief moles are not blind, but are color blind and have poor vision.
